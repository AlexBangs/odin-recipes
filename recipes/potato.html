Air-fried Potato!

Do you want to have “No thoughts, empty head” while cooking?

Are you an anime character named Sasha?

Do you want optimal nutrition without having much effort.

Well then, this recipe is for you!

Ingredients

  • 1 Potato
  • 1 tsp of Olive Oil
  • Pepper to taste
  • Pepper to taste

Tools

  • Aluminum Foil
  • Air Fryer

Instructions

  1. Wash and dry your potato and place under aluminum foil apply salt and pepper all over the potato
  2. Drizzle olive oil on potato and roll potato in seasoning + oil securely wrap aluminum foil around potato
  3. Place potato into air fryer for 50 minutes at 400 degrees F(reedom units).

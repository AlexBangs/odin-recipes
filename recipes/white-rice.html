Perfect White Rice


Have you always wanted beautiful, fluuuuffy rice?

Well then, I got the juice for you!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of white rice
  • 3 cups of water
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1 Tsp of Olive Oil

Tools

  • Rice cooker

Instructions

  1. Put two scoops of white rice in the rice cooker bowl, fill it with water
  2. Wash the rice, A LOT. Like really a lot.
  3. Unironically, to preference(mine is when the water is clear), stop washing and add Salt, pepper, and olive oil
  4. Press your "White Rice" button on your cooker and wait for the ding

Air fried pork chop

This dish literally changed my life.
Unironically, this will save you from the pain
and sufering of having strangers touch your meat

Sussy words aside, behold the recipe!

Ingredients

  • Pork Chop variant of choice
  • 1 tsp of Salt
  • 2 tsp of Pepper
  • 1 tsp of MSG
  • 1 tsp of Cayene Pepper
  • 2 tsp of Unsalted Butter

Required tools

  • Hands! At least one
  • A knife, or suitable substitude for spreading
  • A cutting board to let your meat sit
  • A fridge for your meat to chill in

Instructions for the cullinary elite

  1. Score your washed pork chop. Coat it with
    the listed seasonings and cover it with butter
    Let it chill for at least 30 minutes
  2. After resting, put in pre-heated Air-fryer at 400 degrees
    Flip over when both sides are browned to your liking
  3. Let the meat sit and serve with garnishes and
    hors d'oeuvre I cannot afford

Instructions!

  1. Thoroughly wash your pork chop.
    Make sure you absorb ALL the moisture before continuing to the next step
  2. Score your meat at a 45 degree angle for great flavor
    and social media pictures if that's your thing
  3. Season your food in the order listed above.
    With a gloved, or otherwise sanctioned hand get your meat out
    And sensually rub your porker until it is nicely coated with seasoning
    on both sides.
  4. Coat both sides of your porker with the butter
  5. The hardest step: let your seasoned beauty marinate for at least 30 minutes
  6. Take your beautious meat out of the freezer and into
    an airfryer pre-heated at 400 degrees F(freedom units!)
  7. After about eight minutes
    (plus or minus a few based on the THICC-ness of your meat)
    Flip over your meat and let another 8 minutes elapse.
  8. Take your glorious creation out of the air fryer
    and let it sit to cool down it's thirst
  9. Plate and enjoy!
